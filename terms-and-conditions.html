Failed Messages — Credit Protection

A message is classified as "Failed" when it is rejected before delivery. Common reasons include:

  • Invalid or unregistered recipient phone number
  • Recipient has blocked the WhatsApp Business number
  • WhatsApp API rate limiting or gateway errors
  • Template not approved or rejected by Meta
  • Network or infrastructure failure on Meta's end

In all such cases, no credits will be deducted from your prepaid wallet for that specific message.
